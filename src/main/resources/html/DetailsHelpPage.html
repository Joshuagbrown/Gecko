My Details Help Page

Details page info and functionality:

Once a user has signed in, the details page will be accessible.

General

The General page allows you to edit your own data, such as name, username, and home address.
Also on the general page you are able to view, add, edit, and delete personal vehicles

Register Vehicle:

Clicking the register vehicle button on the left, or the add new vehicle button on the general page will allow you to register a new vehicle.
To register a vehicle, you must know the make, model, year, and charger type of your vehicle.
Once a vehicle has been registered, it is saved to your account.

Saved Journeys:

Clicking the saved journeys button shows you the journeys you have decided to save. Initially this table will be empty, however as you save journeys this will become populated.
You are able to show saved journeys on the map, and deleted journeys that you have saved.

Logout:
